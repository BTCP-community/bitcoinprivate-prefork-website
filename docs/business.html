What is Bitcoin Private?

Bitcoin Private is a cryptocurrency focused on private transactions. It provides the security of a public blockchain, while senders, recipients and other transactional metadata remain unidentifiable. Bitcoin Private is a new project, and it is based on Bitcoin and ZClassic.

Why does Privacy matter?

The biggest advantage of Bitcoin Private will likely not be a surprise: it’s focused on privacy. In Bitcoin, and many other cryptocurrencies, transactions are public. This means that anyone who knows your address - which one will need to send you funds - can see all the transactions that have something to do with that address. If you’re running a business, you will likely not want all your financial data to be out in the open. Bitcoin Private ensures that transactions are private.
