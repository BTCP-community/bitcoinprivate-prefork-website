What is Bitcoin Private?

Bitcoin Private is a cryptocurrency focused on private transactions. It provides the security of a public blockchain, while senders, recipients and other transactional metadata remain unidentifiable. Bitcoin Private is a new project, and it is based on Bitcoin and ZClassic.

Why does Privacy matter?

The biggest advantage of Bitcoin Private will likely not be a surprise: it’s focused on privacy. In Bitcoin, and many other cryptocurrencies, transactions are public. This means that anyone who knows your address - which one will need to send you funds - can see all the transactions that have something to do with that address. If you’re running a business, you will likely not want all your financial data to be out in the open. Bitcoin Private ensures that transactions are private.

How does cryptocurrency & Bitcoin Private help my business?

As you may have noticed in the past months, cryptocurrency is very popular right now. Bitcoin Private features many benefits, compared to traditional systems:

Private

Your transactions are your transactions only. Other parties, such as competitors, are not invited.

Decentralized

There is no central party in charge of your funds. You are in charge.

Community Driven

This is a coin that is owned, and driven by the community.

Fast

Traditional systems can be very slow; transactions with Bitcoin Private can occur in seconds.

Affordable

It can be expensive to send funds with traditional systems. With Bitcoin Private, it is possible to transfer funds, no matter how large the amounts are, much cheaper than with traditional systems.

In Demand

With the surge of cryptocurrency, the market is eagerly anticipating businesses to get involved. Gain a competitive advantage by adopting cryptocurrency and Bitcoin Private.
